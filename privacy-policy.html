Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2025

Overview

Eye Exam Sketchpad is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built our app to ensure that principle is upheld.

Data Collection

Eye Exam Sketchpad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or patient information. All data is stored temporarily in the device's memory only and is cleared when you reset the app or force close it.

Speech Recognition

The app uses on-device speech recognition provided by Apple's Speech framework. No audio recordings or transcripts are sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

User Corrections

The only data that persists between sessions are your speech recognition corrections, which contain no personal information. These are stored locally on your device to improve dictation accuracy.
